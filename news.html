2nd October 2023

New episode of the podcast "The Tale of Grunge"

In this episode we tell the story of the legendary band "Soundgarden", little-known facts, and also listen to unreleased songs and demos. Podcast guest - band founder, Kim Thayil

He will talk about the band's first concerts, how much the lives of the band members changed after the advent of their first popularity, and what awaits grunge in the future.

The Must-Have Synthesizer of 2023: Korg Opsix SE FM Synth

Following on from their Wavestate SE announcement, Korg have done what many people were asking for last week! They’ve given the 61-note keyboard SE treatment to their digital 6-operator FM synth, the Opsix, greatly expanding both the power and performance capability of their DX-7-inspired instrument.

You can already try this synthesizer in our studio "Antarctica"
