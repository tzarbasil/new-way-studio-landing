Our studios

Studio "A"

It was conceived by us as a completely analog studio, related to the classic studios of the 50s and 60s. It is great for recording various musical instruments, as well as vocals.

New Way Studio

For you, we have equipped a large number of synthesizers, drum machines, DJ consoles, as well as digital programs. EDM, Techno, any other electronic music is created here!

Green Fields Studio

A small cozy studio is suitable for those who like to retire and completely immerse themselves in work. If you are a beginner musician, then it is perfect for your first steps in the field of recording.

Wood Heaven Studio

It is in this studio that a variety of noise and sound effects are born, which we can hear in completely different areas: from films to video games.д

Antarctica Studio

Our most modern studio, equipped with the latest technology. The futuristic interior will inspire you to new musical exploits, and a large selection of various electronic musical instruments will give you the opportunity to experiment

Timeless Classic Studio

One of our first studios, which has survived to this day practically unchanged. Great sound and time-tested equipment!
