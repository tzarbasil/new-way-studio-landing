About Us

The studio was founded in 1987 by sound engineers Daniel Clarke and Arthur Milgett and relied on recording electronic and alternative music, gradually expanding its capabilities.

Starting with recording underground artists of various types of music, the studio became more popular among more famous artists. We are proud of the hundreds of songs and albums made in our studio. We were the first to dare to take a bold step in the form of using analog equipment to record electronic compositions. Thanks to this unusual combination, the songs acquired the signature sound of New Way Studios

We've been experimenting for over 30 years and getting better with every recording, giving you the best quality.

The New Wave Studios team are true professionals who have worked with the most famous artists in the most famous studios, such as Sound City and Abbey Road. You can always trust them to work on your composition.

Our priority is the best sound you've ever heard.
