About Us

New Way Studios was founded in 1987 and has always followed the highest standards of sound recording quality.

We elegantly combined modern technologies with analog equipment, which is why you can always recognize the signature sound of our studio.

The studio does not focus on recording artists of a certain genre. We carry out high-quality work of any complexity

Our team consists of certified sound engineers and mastering engineers, so you can be confident in the quality of our services.
